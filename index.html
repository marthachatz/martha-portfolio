  • myDesk

    Website


    Overview

    myDesk is a cloud application primarily used by employers for employees to clock in and out digitally when entering and leaving the workplace.



    Problem statement

    Since it was launched alongside the mandatory use of digital cards, they wanted to quickly and easily acquire subscribers through Google advertising.


    Research and Discovery

    I created an advertising account on Google AdWords and conducted research to gather the appropriate keywords that the target audience was most searching for at the time. I then crafted relevant ad copy to maximize the chances of a CTA. Additionally, I identified negative keywords to avoid the ads appearing in unrelated searches. I created two campaigns, one search and one display (retargeting).


    Examples




    Reflection

    During the creation and promotion of the campaign for the MyDesk.gr application, I had the opportunity to dive deep into managing and strategizing the advertising of a specialized product. One of the most important lessons I learned from this experience was the importance of understanding the specific target audience, which in this case, were businesses with staff requiring time tracking. During the initial keyword research, I paid particular attention to terms related to workforce management. Additionally, I discovered the value of simplicity and clarity in the advertising message. I designed ads that emphasized the ease of use and effectiveness of the application, which resonated very positively with the audience. While analyzing the campaign data, I recognized the need for continuous optimization. I identified keywords that were underperforming and replaced them with more relevant and effective choices.
